This is a reluctant Steve Jobs fansite

Steve Jobs was the co-founder and two-time CEO of Apple Inc. Undoubtedly a brillient businessman, Steve is also considered to be on of the greatest tech visionaries of our time.

Steve's life story is nothing short of fascinating. After dropping out of college, he joined forces with his friend Steve Wozniak to start Apple in 1976. A year later, the duo gained wealth and fame thanks to the Apple II, one of the first highly successful mass-produced personal computers. This was followed by be the hugely successful Macintosh in 1984, the first mass-produced computer with a Graphical User Interface (GUI). After being ousted from Apple in 1985, Steve went on to oversee the creation of 2 new companies: NeXT and Pixar. The success of the movie Toy Story made Steve a billionaire.

In 1997, Steve returned to Apple, with the company on the brink of extinction. Nonetheless, Steve was a tremendous salesman, and was able to use his business acumen to slowly turn Apple's fortunes around and bring the company back to profitibility.
