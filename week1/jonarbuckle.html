This is the home page of Jon Arbuckle, a cartoon character who owns a cat named Garfield.

Quentin "Jon" Arbuckle is a fictional character from the Garfield comic strip by Jim Davis.
He has also appeared in the animated television series Garfield and Friends, the computer-animated The Garfield Show, and two live-action/computer-animated feature films.

Here are some useful things about Jon Arbuckle

  • Jon's birthday is July 28
  • He can play accordion, guitar, and bongos and sing
  • His favorite music style is polka
